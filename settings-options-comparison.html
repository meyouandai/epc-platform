Alternative Approaches

Approach A: Lead Delivery Method

Single question: "How would you like to receive new leads?" with options for Email only, SMS only, or Both. Keeps it simple and focused on core function.

Approach B: Notification Categories

Group by purpose: Lead Delivery, Account Alerts, System Messages. Each category can have email/SMS toggles.

Approach C: Smart Defaults

Minimal settings: Default to email for everything, only show SMS toggle for urgent items. Reduces cognitive load.

Approach D: No Settings Page

Zero configuration: Email for leads (required), SMS opt-in during onboarding, spending limits in billing section only.
