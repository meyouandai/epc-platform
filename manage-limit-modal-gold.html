Analysis: The Problem with Too Many Options

Version 1 (Full List): 9 options is overwhelming. Users will likely experience choice paralysis.

Version 2 (Grouped): Better organization but still shows all options. The grouping helps but doesn't solve the core problem.

Version 3 (Smart Defaults) ‚≠ê: Best approach - shows relevant options (nearby limits + upgrade path) with ability to see more if needed. Most users won't need Bronze limits at Gold level.

Version 4 (Tabbed): Clean separation of billing types, with simplified quick-select options.

Recommendation:

Use Smart Defaults approach - show 3-4 relevant limits based on current level, with an expandable option for power users who need specific amounts. This prevents overwhelming Gold users with Bronze ¬£50-¬£150 limits they'll never use.
