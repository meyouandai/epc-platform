KEY INSIGHTS

What Assessors Actually Want to See:

1. Current State:
  • How much can I spend? (threshold)
  • How much have I spent? (current spend)
  • What level am I? (Bronze/Silver/Gold)
2. Available Actions:
  • What thresholds can I upgrade to?
  • What thresholds can I downgrade to?
  • Can I unlock new level benefits?
3. NOT Interested In:
  • Historical payment counts (if already eligible)
  • Lead count estimates (variable pricing)
  • Abstract "trust building" concepts
