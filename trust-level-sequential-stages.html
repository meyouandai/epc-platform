Key Sequential Design Principles

Progressive Disclosure

  • Manage Limit button only appears when options exist
  • Users learn about next levels gradually
  • No overwhelming choices upfront

Risk Management

  • Maximum £50 exposure for new users
  • Proven payment history required for increases
  • Users can downgrade anytime

User Control

  • Always show current threshold clearly
  • Eligibility shown when available
  • Educational "Learn More" always present

Business Growth

  • System grows with user's business
  • Higher levels offer real cash flow benefits
  • Professional features for established users
