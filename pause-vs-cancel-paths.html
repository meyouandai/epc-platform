üéØ Strategic Recommendations

Pause Path: Minimal friction. Users have clear intent to return, so focus on helpful alternatives rather than pressure. Build goodwill for their return.
Cancel Path: High stakes require more intervention. Use progressive disclosure - start reasonable, escalate strategically. Every retained customer is worth the effort.
Balance: Both paths must feel respectful. Even aggressive retention should offer genuine value, not just delay tactics.
